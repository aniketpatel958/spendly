Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Spendly ("we", "us") collects when you use our personal expense tracking application (the "Service"), how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the Service, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide directly when you create an account and use the Service. This includes your account details, such as your name and email address, and the expense data you enter — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We may also collect limited technical information, such as basic log data, that is generated automatically when you interact with the Service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Service. This means storing your expenses so you can view and manage them, generating summaries and category breakdowns, and maintaining the security of your account. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use your financial information for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored so that it remains available to you across sessions. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials confidential.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on third party services and infrastructure to operate — for example, hosting providers that store data on our behalf. These providers are only given the information necessary to perform their functions and are expected to protect it. We do not share your personal expense data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the Service. We will do our best to address your concerns and respond to any requests regarding your personal information.
